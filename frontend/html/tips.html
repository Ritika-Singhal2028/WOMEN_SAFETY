Safety Tips

Share Your Location

Always share your live location with trusted contacts when traveling alone.

Keep Phone Charged

Ensure your phone is charged and has enough balance for emergency calls.

Stay Alert

Avoid distractions like headphones in isolated areas.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, leave immediately and seek help.

Check Transport Details

Note vehicle number before entering cabs or autos.

Stay in Lit Areas

Prefer well-lit and crowded places while walking at night.
